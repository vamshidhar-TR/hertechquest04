Use case (the tax professional) Voice layer (in starter/) Your backend
It begins and ends with the use case. The tax professional never leaves the workpaper — they ask aloud and the cited answer comes back to them. Between those two moments: everything before stage 4 is the voice layer (given to you); everything after is your backend, reached through one function — answer(question). Swap what's behind it (CoCounsel over MCP, a REST API, a local model) without touching the voice UI. No answer ships without a citation.
